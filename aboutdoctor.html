About Dr.Sarath Chandra

Dr.Sarath Chandra is the Best Vascular Doctor in India. He is a famous vascular Surgeon in India. He has done MBBS (Hons) from Dr NTR University, Guntur Medical College, Guntur, 2006-2012. He did MS (General Surgery): Jss University, Jss Medical College, Mysuru, 2014-2017. MCh (Vascular and Endovascular Surgery): Dr.MGR University, Government Stanely Medical College, Chennai, 2019-2022.

Dr. Sarath is an expert in a broad spectrum of vascular procedures, includingAV access creationfor dialysis and managing access-related complications through AV transpositions and grafts. He specializes in treatingvaricose veinsusing cutting-edge methods likeendovenous ablation(radiofrequency, laser, glue, foam sclerotherapy). Dr. Sarath also excels indeep vein thrombosis management, vascular trauma care, and both endovascular andopen treatmentsfor peripheral vascular disease. His expertise extends to complex aortic surgeries,aneurysm repair(open and EVAR/TEVAR),carotid endarterectomy,and treating congenital vascular malformations and iatrogenic injuries.

Dr.Sarath Chandra treats all kinds of vascular Disorders "Arterial Disease Treatment" “Venous Disease Treatment”, “Lymphatic Disease Treatment”, “Trauma and Emergency Vascular Services” to name a few. He is always involved in the treatment of his patients and gives enough time to them during consultations. Currently he has been working as a vascular and endovascular in AIG . That’s why most of the people has say he is the best Vascular surgeon in Hderabad . He has been trained in prestigious hospitals in India.
